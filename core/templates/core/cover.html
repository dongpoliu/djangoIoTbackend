Support for geographically distributed researchers

Django IoT Mgmt Backend is a web server for IoT device.

Publish extra information

Normally you can't publish all relevant information about the research as frequently there is a limit amount of pages to publish your article on a certain conference or journal. Django IoT Mgmt Backend provides you a beautiful interface to publish any extra information like the whole result from the search string, all modifications of the search string, more details about the protocol, etc.

Support for tertiary studies

Having a huge amount of Systematic Literature Reviews in the platform, it will provide a powerful support for tertiary studies, that is, systematic literature review of secondary studies.
